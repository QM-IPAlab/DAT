Abstract

We address the Global–Local Feature Imbalance in VLMs. Existing models struggle with cropped region proposals.

We introduce Decoupled Adaptivity Training (DAT). By using in-pipeline pseudo-labels and a sigmoid-based contrastive loss, we allow the detector to "teach itself."

Result: State-of-the-art performance on COCO & LVIS with minimal training cost.
